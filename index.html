TOO ORDINARY FOR REWIRE ----------- MUSIC DIRECTORY

The music tool. In late 1980s vinyl, cassette tapes and compact discs were the main formats of distributing music. It would be difficult to get your hands on physical recordings of underground noise music, such as the music that VITAL is about. The arrival of internet changed all this. TOOL #2 jumps the gap between the physical magazine and the music by embedding the graphics of the magazine with information. Hover over them to see for yourself and listen.
